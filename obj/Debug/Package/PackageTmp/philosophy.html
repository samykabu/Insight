Philosophy

 
 

Insight applies a revolutionary method in career counseling in which the client is the determiner of his own future. Insight does not believe in psychometric testing and giving corresponding digits for skills and attributes. Insight counselors believe that the best person who understands oneself is himself, and our counselors assist the person in this quest for self actualization.

 

The Insight test “gages not measures” 40 different aspects ranging from spatial and emotional intelligence to anger threshold, social skills, and various other competencies. This test will assist the counselor in the remainder portions of the program. In the one on one sessions, the counselor distills the person from external pressures like peer, family, social, and market pressures to find the individual’s true passion in life. Upon which, the counselor facilitates the person in meeting experts in his chosen field to get an insider’s view. Then the counselor will coordinate visits/and or rotations in the field to gain a tangible feel of field. All this is essential in underlining the passion and ensuring that the chosen field is the right choice.

Insight realizes that this human-based approach is both time consuming and requires more effort from us. We however have a sound belief that delivering a quality service based on strong moral, ethical, and professional values is the cornerstone Insight bases its reputation on. We believe each person deserves the utmost attention, time, and care no matter what. This is why a big portion of our client base comes from referrals from other satisfied Insight clients

 
 
 
 

Applying Insight to HR

 

People who apply for jobs usually send their CV’s to various entities hoping to get an interview. They highlight certain aspects in each CV hoping to get the attention of a particular industry. A graduate would highlight investment skills for example when applying to a bank, while highlighting social skills when applying to a hospital. This “CV tweaking” is very damaging to the applicant and company because he is not looking for the right place, he is just look for a place right now.

 
Insight solves this problem because a person that enters the program is neutral to us. We do not specify an industry with him and have no

 

 

recruitment target at a certain company affecting our service to the client. When a person finishes the program, he decides his passion and therefore fits the industry and job description resulting from the program. Therefore when we forward him to an entity, they know that he fits the job mentally, psychologically, and emotionally and 90% of the interview would already have been done. The company will only then discuss details of logistics with him such as salary and so forth (10% of interview).This causes higher retention, better job satisfaction, and increased productivity for both the employee and the company
