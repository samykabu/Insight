Programs
 
 

Insight is a career counseling, training, and recruitment firm focused on helping students, employees, and job seekers to find their passion in life and enable them to achieve it. Insight applies new-age, individually personalized programs tested and praised by world-renowned psychologists, universities, and human resource firms which are revolutionary in helping individuals find career success and job.


Insight helps students and employees select their careers themselves by giving them the tools they need through a step by step counseling program. Insight is non-automated and is distinctly different in both approach and methodology. A developmental psychologist will be with the individual every step of the way to assist in career selection. Hundreds of satisfied clients of all ages are well on their way in their careers of passion thanks to Insight’s approach to bring the “H” respectfully back to “HR”.
