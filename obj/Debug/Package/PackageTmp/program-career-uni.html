Career Counseling/Recruitment - College Students

 
 

 

Program Includes:

 

  • The Insight test: a single test to understand (not measure) various mental aspects such as IQ, EQ, spatial intelligence, leadership, and others

    •  

  • Relating test results to Academic performance to find patterns

    •  

  • One on one sessions to distill students from external pressures and find their true passion

    •  

  • Comparing passion to potential (i.e. results from test to that of sessions) ex: Spatial Intelligence/ architecture

    •  

  • Meeting with an expert in the chosen field to inform student on his career

    •  

  • Tangible application of career choice by volunteering in the specified work field

    •  

  • Career plan detailing every aspect of career path from college to the work force

    •  

  • Graduate School application assistance and/or recruitment where we find a job for the student
