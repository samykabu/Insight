About Us

   
 
 
  • Insight careers is a limited liability company (LLC) registered in Saudi Arabia. Insight was a brainchild of a psychology researcher at Stanford

    •  

  • The research matured to become the cornerstone of its career counseling program patented in the United States

    •  

  • The researcher is co-founder and partner at Insight and, along with a team of capable psychologists, continues to develop career oriented programs aimed at enhancing peoples’ academic and work life

 

Vision

To be the main regional catalyst of career success for all ages

 

Mission

To enable people to successfully pursue their careers of passion
 

 
 

 

 

Goals

 
1. Youth

Enhance Academic experience, develop skills, and guide them in the pursuit of their respective careers of passion

 
 
 
2. People

Ignite their passion and put them on the right career path

 
 
 
3. Corporations

Enhance their selection process and evolve prospective company leaders

 
 
 
4. Society
Unleash the potential of people from all walks of life
 

Objectives
 

 
1. Regarding Youth
  • Discover children’s talents and interests, and provide a nurturing environment for them to grow
  • Provide career counseling to high-school students to help them find passions and apply them
  • Provide support services/counseling to college students to enhance academics and success
2. Regarding People
  • Assist them in solving underlying problems, obstacles, fears, and other aspects hindering career
  • Assist them in finding their life passion
  • Assist in applying a career plan to reach their goals
3. Regarding Corporations
  • Headhunt talent and forward them to company
  • Filter applicants applying to company
  • Develop future leaders by addressing individual passion, skills, and potential
4. Regarding Society
  • Give support to current charities, development projects, and recruitment initiatives
  • Organize events to inspire and engage
  • Increase awareness of passion in career paths
 
 
 

Values

 
1. We believe in individualism, and therefore:
  • Every person is an individual and has individual goals
  • Every individual has the right to pursue his/her goals
  • Every individual has the internal strength to fulfill his/her goals
  • All information entrusted to us by our clients will be kept safe
2. Every client has lifelong access to our service, and therefore:
  • We will make available our services throughout the individuals career
  • We will provide a range of service affordable to all
 
3. The value of our services are strictly dependent on our employees, and therefore:
  • We will seek, employ, and retain only those who share our passion
  • We will deliver quality through the knowledge and professionalism of our staff
